Preventing Threats

Here are some tips to preventing the threats on this site. These could be a life-saver if someone tries to use them against you in the future. Use them!

Top Tips

Biometrics

Alternative authorisation rather than username/password combination. It uses your physical body to verify you are who you say you are. Face ID, Touch ID, etc. are examples of this.
